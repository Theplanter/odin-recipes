Crazy Delicious Potato Soup Recipe

This is by far the most simple, delicious, and easiest potato soup recipe I have made.

Ingredients

  • 4lb of potatoes
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of spinach
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • 4 cloves of garlic "minced"
  • seasoning of your choosing
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tbsp of flour
  • 2 tbsp of butter

instructions

  1. cut your potatoes into 1/2 inch
  2. melt butter in pot
    put your onions in and cook them until brown
  3. after the onions are brown put all the Ingredients into the pot except the milk and flour
  4. bring the pot to boil and stir the pot regularly
  5. in a small bowl put your milk and flour together and mix them together
  6. when the potatoes are soft
    put in the slurry you just made into the pot
  7. Enjoy!!
