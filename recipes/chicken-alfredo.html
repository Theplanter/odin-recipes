Creamy Chicken Alfredo Recipe

This is one of the easiest, and creamiest chicken pasta recipes you will ever make.

Ingredients

  • 1-1 1/2lb of chicken

    • For seasoning on the chicken you can add what you like

  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 pound of noodles you like the most
  • 1/2-1cup of parmesan cheese
  • 2tbsp of butter
  • tsp of salt
  • tsp of pepper
  • 1 tbsp of garlic minced

instructions

  1. mix the seasoning of your choosing to the chicken
    cook chicken until firm and brownish
  2. melt butter into pan
    then slowly pour in your heavy whipping cream
  3. when the pan is slightly boiling
    slowly add the parmesan cheese until sauce is thick
  4. boil a pot water for the noodles
    when the water is boiling put noodles in
  5. when the noodles are done
    mix the sauce and noodles together
