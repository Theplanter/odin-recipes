Fancy Italian Bread Recipe

Are you ready to make the crispiest loaf of bread ever.
come with me on this amazing journey of bread making

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of wheat unbleached flour
  • 2 tsp of instant yeast
  • 2 tsp of sea salt
  • 1 1/2 of warm water

instructions

  1. put together all the dry Ingredients into one bowl and mix them together
  2. slowly add the water and mix together with the dry Ingredients
  3. keep mixing until the dough does not stick to your hand
  4. place towel over the bowl when your done mixing
    let the dough rise for one hour
  5. turn over on for 400Degs
  6. grease up your cooking sheet then cook your dough
